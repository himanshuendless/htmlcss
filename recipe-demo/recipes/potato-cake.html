Old Fashioned Potato Cake

These potato cakes are great with any meal or as a snack. Simple and quick to make with only a few ingredients. Kids of ALL ages love 'em. by DoMonkey
  • Prep: 10 mins
  • Cook: 10 mins
  • Total: 20 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1 egg
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup vegetable oil, or as needed
